What Do iSIT For?

The iSIT movement raises awareness about the benefits of mindfulness and meditation practice for college students.

The practices of meditation, mindfulness, and compassion training are greatly needed for students in the high-stress university environment. Student mental health is becoming increasingly worse. Today there are typically two options for students who experience stress and anxiety, or struggle with ADHD or depression: medication or counseling services. Research indicates that meditation and mindfulness practice can be a positive alternative to these more traditional, and sometimes limiting, options. Together, we can help each other develop quality meditation and mindfulness groups that are conducive for improving the well-being of college students. iSIT, as an ongoing process of learning, is working to empower students to take control of their issues by bringing themselves to these group practices.
